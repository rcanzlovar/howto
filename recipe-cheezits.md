# home made cheezits 

Preheat 300 degree oven

* 1 lb sharp cheddar, frozen and shredded
* 2 cups flour
* 1-2 tbs kosher salt
* 1/2 stick of butter

Water as needed to make dough (just a spash or two) 

Roll into sheet as thin as you can. 

Cut into squares. 

Cook at 300 degrees, 20 minutes.
