# costco chocolate cookie recipe

Preheat oven to 350 degree F

* 1 cup salted butter
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1/2 cup white sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1/3 cup corn syrup
* 1 tbsp molasses
* 1/2 tsp vanilla exttract
* 3 1/2 cups flour
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp baking soda
* 1 1/3 cup chocolate chunks

* cook 11 minutes in 350 degree oven
